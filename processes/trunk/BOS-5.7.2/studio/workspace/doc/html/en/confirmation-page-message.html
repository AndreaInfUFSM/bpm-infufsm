Change confirmation page message

(Category: Forms Modeling)

The confirmation message can be shown whenever a series of forms is completed by an end user, and a process case moves on to the next task(s), performed by another end user. That is, at the end of a sequence of tasks/steps performed by one end user, a confirmation message is shown and the subsequent step shows up ready for the next user.

This is what the user would see on submission of the final page of a form (for example, "thank you for your order").
