Add a connector to a task or a pool

(Category: Connectors)

Connectors can be added to an individual task, or to the process level (that is, to a pool). The configuration options are slightly different, but generally similar. The first configuration wizard asks for the same type of information for any connector.

To add a connector provided in Bonita Open Solution to a task or to a pool, click on the task or pool and go to its Details pane. Them, under the general tab, click on the connectors pane. Click on Add.

Note: you will need to have global variables defined, to associate with your inputs and/or output results.

When the “Select a connector” wizard appears, choose the type of connector you want to add. These connectors are each described in their specific sections of the documentation, but all of them go through the same first steps.

When you have completed these first two wizards to add a connector, the next wizards are presented to define the specific parameters required for each connector's inputs and, where appropriate, for its outputs.
