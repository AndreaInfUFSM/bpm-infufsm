Define and assign actors

(Category: Process Modeling)

The term actor in Bonita Open Solution is defined as a subset or part of the organization defined for a process. An actor represents a group of individuals authorized or assigned to perform specific tasks in the process. When the process is deployed, one or more individuals will then be assigned a step to complete.

There are three required steps to completely define actor selection and assignment:

  1. Define the actor
  2. Assign the actor to a lane and [optionally] change the actor for a specific task(s) in a lane
  3. Map the actor to the organization

Define actors at the process (pool) level, and then assign them to lanes and/or tasks. A lane or task can have only a single actor.
