Choose a look-and-feel

(Category: Look and Feel)

Look'n'feel is the name given to the features used to customize the appearance of web applications. There are some default Look'n'feel templates available to be customized, or a new look'n'feel can be uploaded.

Overall look-n-feel and specific page templates can be applied to, for example:

  • the process instantiation form (entry form)
  • error messages
  • the log-in page
  • confirmation pages

Custom CSS can also be applied to form templates.
