Choose color and font

(Category: Appearance)

Customize the appearance of pools and tasks by choosing your own color and font. The available options to change are :

  • the font type
  • the font size
  • the font color
  • the font in bold
  • the font in italic
  • the background color of the pool or task
  • the outline color of the pool or task
  • applying an existing style copied from another pool or task

You can also change the appearance of a task or pool by applying the style copied from another one.
